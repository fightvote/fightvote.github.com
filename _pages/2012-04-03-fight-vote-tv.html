Vote on MMA fights, compete against your friends from anywhere in the world.

Fight Vote TV allows you to vote on MMA fights from your smart phone, and compete against your friends watching the fight, whether they are in the same room, or in another country.

To play:

  • On a desktop computer?  Go to blog.fightvote.com and vote on any matchups along side expert commentary on upcoming MMA matchups.
  • On your smart phone?  Go directly to vote.fightvote.com and vote on matchups from anywhere.
  • During the fight, your score will be updated in real time.  If you picked the winner of the matchup, you'll get points for that based on the Fight Vote algorithm.  See our scoring rules here.
  • Watching the fight at a sports bar?  Ask them to install the Fight Vote TV application and watch your score against your friends in the bar.
  • Watching the fight at home?  Install the Fight Vote TV application and watch your score updated in real time.
  • Paying attention to the fight from your smart phone?  Login to vote.fightvote.com and watch your score updated in real time.
