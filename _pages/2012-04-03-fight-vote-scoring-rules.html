Fight Vote Scoring Rules

Points are awarded for:

  • Matchup: 10 pts.  Picking the correct matchup.  You get ten points for choosing the correct outcome of a matchup.
  • Underdog matchup bonus:  3 points.  If you pick a matchup which is an "extreme underdog" (where 66% of the people voting choose the opposite direction) you get the underdog bonus for that matchup.
  • Bonus matchup: 6 pts.  One matchup is secretly designated before the card as the bonus matchup.  Correctly picking this matchup gives you an extra 6 points.

Example scoring:

  • If there are ten matchups, and you successfully pick 6 of the fights, you would get 60 points ( (6 x 10) == 60 ).
  • If there are ten matchups, and you successfully pick 6 of them, and two are "extreme underdog" matchups, and you correctly pick one of these, then you would have 63 points  ( (6 x 10) + (1 x 3) == 63 ).
  • If there are ten matchups, and you successfully pick 6 of them, and you successfully picked two extreme underdog matchups, and you picked the third matchup on the card which was the bonus matchup, then you would have 72 points ( (6 x 10) + (2 x 3) + 6 == 72 ).

Fight Vote reserves the right to change scoring rules at any time.
